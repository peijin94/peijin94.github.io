    I am a solar physicist specializing in solar radio astronomy, electromagnetic wave propagation, and space weather. My research focuses on understanding solar activities through low-frequency radio observations, utilizing cutting-edge radio telescopes to study solar radio bursts and their propagation through the inner heliosphere.
